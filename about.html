About Us

Welcome to Rail Rover! We are a railway reservation site dedicated to providing seamless ticket booking experiences. "Destination to your tickets"!

About the Project

Our project involves creating a comprehensive web-based system for railway ticket reservations, capturing essential journey and passenger details, including meal preferences. Utilizing advanced front-end tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, we ensure a seamless, intuitive, and user-friendly booking experience. The system efficiently confirms reservations, providing a streamlined, reliable, and convenient service for travelers.
